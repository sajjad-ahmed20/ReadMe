## Featured Projects

### 🌍 [GHG Emissions Intelligence Calculator](https://github.com/sajjad-ahmed20/ghg-emissions-calculator)
An end-to-end GHG emissions platform built in Python with an interactive Power BI dashboard — covering Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions, aligned with GHG Protocol Corporate Standard and India's BRSR Principle 6.

- 24 emission factors · 7 activity categories · Net Zero modelling to 2050
- 3-scenario pathway analysis — Business as Usual, Moderate Action, Aggressive Action
- BRSR Principle 6 disclosure table — annual report ready
- Sources: GHG Protocol · CEA India · UK DEFRA · EXIOBASE

### 📋 [CSRD-ESRS & GRI Framework Mapping](https://github.com/sajjad-ahmed20/esrs-gri-framework-mapping)
Comparative analysis of ESRS E1 (Climate Change) against GRI Standards (GRI 302, GRI 305) — mapping 10+ climate data points, identifying disclosure gaps, and analysing compliance implications for companies transitioning between frameworks.

### 📊 [Reliance Industries Climate Risk Assessment](https://github.com/sajjad-ahmed20/reliance-climate-risk-analysis)
End-to-end Climate & Business Risk Assessment across 5+ years of financial and strategic data — transition risk, capital allocation risk, stranded asset exposure, and staged investment recommendations under Net Zero scenarios.
